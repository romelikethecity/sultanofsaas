Tidio Pricing (2026)

Starting at Free / $29+/mo

Tidio is priced starting at Free / $29+/mo, sitting mid-range in help desk. It scores 6.8/10 in our overall review. This page unpacks what each plan actually gets you, what the real monthly spend looks like at different team sizes, and where Tidio's pricing earns its keep or fails to.

The quick read on Tidio: AI chatbot for e-commerce and small business websites. Tidio's Lyro AI chatbot can answer common questions automatically, and the live chat handles everything the bot can't. It's popular with Shopify and WooCommerce stores. The free tier is limited but functional for very small sites.

Tidio Plans Explained

Each tier in plain English. What unlocks at each level, and when to upgrade.

Free — 50 conversations/mo

Free is priced at 50 conversations/mo. Check the vendor page for current terms before signing up.

Starter — $29/mo

At $29/mo, Starter is the tier Tidio wants you to pick. It fills the gaps in the entry plan and adds the integrations and automation that most teams discover they need in week two. Budget for this from day one if you're serious about using Tidio.

Growth — $59/mo

Growth costs $59/mo. It's a step-up from the base plan, typically with more automation, higher limits, or advanced integrations. Worth it only if you can point to a specific feature on this tier that you actually need.

Tidio+ — $499/mo

At $499/mo, Tidio+ is the ceiling of the self-serve pricing. It bundles in the features teams ask for after they hit scale. Good value if you actually use them, expensive padding if you don't.

What's Included in Tidio Pricing

Every plan includes the core Tidio feature set. Here's what you get access to on paid tiers:

  • Live chat
  • AI chatbot (Lyro)
  • Ticketing
  • Email marketing
  • Shopify integration
  • Visitor tracking

Feature depth grows with the tier. Entry plans cap on automation, integrations, or usage limits. Upper plans unlock the heavier features that mid-market teams actually need. Read the vendor's feature matrix before picking a tier, especially if one specific feature is the reason you're buying.

What to Watch Out For

The most common pricing complaints buyers raise about Tidio:

  • AI chatbot credits are limited and expensive to scale
  • Advanced features require $59+/mo plans
  • Not a full help desk, more of a chat + bot tool

None of these are deal-breakers on their own. They're the things you want to negotiate or plan around before you sign a contract. The worst time to discover an add-on fee is month three.

The Sultan's Verdict on Tidio Pricing

Tidio scores 6.8/10, which is a reminder that the price tag isn't the whole picture. You're paying Free / $29+/mo for a product with real limitations, and the cons matter. Before committing, check the alternatives above. At this score, you need a specific reason to pick Tidio over the leaders in help desk.

The fit test is simple. Tidio is built for e-commerce stores and small websites that want an ai chatbot + live chat. If that's you, the pricing is worth it. If it's not, you'll end up paying for features you never touch while missing features you actually need. Buy the tool that fits your motion, not the one with the best pricing page.

The bottom line: Tidio's pricing is defensible if you actually use what it's good at. Its biggest strength is lyro ai chatbot resolves common questions automatically, and that's where the money goes. If that strength maps to a real pain point in your business, pay the price. If not, walk away and pick something cheaper.

Tidio Pricing FAQs

How much does Tidio cost?

Tidio has a free plan, and the first paid tier is Starter at $29/mo. Most teams that outgrow the free tier end up on Starter or higher once they hit the free-plan limits.

Is there a free version of Tidio?

Yes. Tidio offers a free plan that covers the basics. It's a real product, not a time-limited trial, so you can run on it indefinitely if your needs stay small.

Does Tidio charge per user?

No, Tidio's Starter plan is a flat platform fee of $29/mo. You can add team members without the per-seat pricing that most SaaS tools use.

Are there hidden costs with Tidio?

The biggest gotcha buyers report: ai chatbot credits are limited and expensive to scale. Read the contract line items before signing, and ask for the full cost including onboarding and add-ons.

Can you negotiate Tidio pricing?

Small teams usually can't move list prices much, but annual commits, multi-seat deals, and end-of-quarter timing all give you room to push back. Ask for an annual discount and any waived onboarding fees before you sign.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Tidio?

The cheapest real alternative is Freshdesk at Free / $15/agent/mo. That's well under Tidio's Free / $29+/mo. Don't switch just for the savings. Compare what you'd give up in our Freshdesk review.
