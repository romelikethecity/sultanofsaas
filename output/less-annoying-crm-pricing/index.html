Less Annoying CRM Pricing (2026)

Starting at $15/user/mo

Simple Pricing — $15/user/mo

At $15 per user per month, Simple Pricing is the real starting line for most paid crm software buyers. A 5-person team lands at $75/mo, a 10-person team at $150/mo. You get the core product, but expect feature caps that push you toward the next tier within 6-12 months of serious use.

What's Included in Less Annoying CRM Pricing

Every plan includes the core Less Annoying CRM feature set. Here's what you get access to on paid tiers:

  • Contact management
  • Pipeline tracking

Feature depth grows with the tier. Entry plans cap on automation, integrations, or usage limits. Upper plans unlock the heavier features that mid-market teams actually need. Read the vendor's feature matrix before picking a tier, especially if one specific feature is the reason you're buying.

What to Watch Out For

The most common pricing complaints buyers raise about Less Annoying CRM:

  • Missing advanced features (automation, AI)
  • No built-in email marketing
  • Limited integrations

None of these are deal-breakers on their own. They're the things you want to negotiate or plan around before you sign a contract. The worst time to discover an add-on fee is month three.

The Sultan's Verdict on Less Annoying CRM Pricing

Less Annoying CRM scores 7.4/10, which is a reminder that the price tag isn't the whole picture. You're paying $15/user/mo for a product with real limitations, and the cons matter. Before committing, check the alternatives above. At this score, you need a specific reason to pick Less Annoying CRM over the leaders in crm software.

The fit test is simple. Less Annoying CRM is built for solo founders and micro-teams who want zero complexity. If that's you, the pricing is worth it. If it's not, you'll end up paying for features you never touch while missing features you actually need. Buy the tool that fits your motion, not the one with the best pricing page.

The bottom line: Less Annoying CRM's pricing is defensible if you actually use what it's good at. Its biggest strength is one simple price: no tiers, no upsells, and that's where the money goes. If that strength maps to a real pain point in your business, pay the price. If not, walk away and pick something cheaper.

Less Annoying CRM Pricing FAQs

How much does Less Annoying CRM cost?

Less Annoying CRM starts at $15/user/mo. The paid plans scale up from there based on features, seats, or usage. Check the pricing table above for the full tier breakdown.

Does Less Annoying CRM have a free trial?

Less Annoying CRM doesn't lead with a free plan, so check the vendor site for current trial terms. Most tools in this category offer a 14-day trial, and some let you demo the product before signing up.

How much does Less Annoying CRM cost for a 10-person team?

On the Simple Pricing plan at $15 per user per month, a 10-person team pays $150/mo ($1,800/year). Add more for higher tiers or usage-based features.

Are there hidden costs with Less Annoying CRM?

Watch for add-on modules, onboarding fees, and minimum contract lengths on annual plans. These are common in this category and often aren't visible on the public pricing page.
