The Sultan's Verdict

ActiveCampaign's automation builder is leagues ahead of Mailchimp's. Any team that needs more than basic drip sequences will outgrow Mailchimp quickly.

Pricing Deep Dive

ActiveCampaign offers 4 tiers: Starter at $29/mo, Plus at $49/mo, Pro at $149/mo, Enterprise at Custom. The entry point sits at $29/mo, which lands it in the mid range for this category.

Mailchimp prices across 4 tiers: Free at $0 (500 contacts), Essentials at $13/mo, Standard at $20/mo, Premium at $350/mo. Starting at Free / $13/mo, it falls into the budget bracket.

When you stack the two side by side, ActiveCampaign at $29/mo vs Mailchimp at Free / $13/mo, the question isn't which costs less. It's which gives you more per dollar for your specific workflow. A cheaper tool that doesn't fit your process costs more in lost productivity than a pricier one that does.

Who Should Choose What

You're watching every dollar. Go with Mailchimp (starting at Free / $13/mo). It covers the core functionality without the sticker shock. You can always upgrade or switch later once you've outgrown it.

You need the deepest feature set. ActiveCampaign takes this one. Its standout strengths include most powerful automation builder in the category and excellent deliverability. If you're willing to pay for completeness, ActiveCampaign won't leave you wanting.
