Informatica CDQ Implementation Notes

Three things to plan for before you sign up for Informatica CDQ:

  • Onboarding time. Budget at least one full week to get Informatica CDQ configured for your team's actual workflow, even if the vendor advertises a 5-minute setup. The 5-minute setup gets you a logged-in account. The week gets you a tool that fits the way you work.
  • Data migration. If you're switching from another tool, plan the import carefully. Field mapping is where most data cleaning migrations break. Run a small test batch (50-100 records) before importing the full dataset, and verify everything lands in the right place.
  • Team training. Even simple tools fail if half your team doesn't use them. Schedule one short training session within the first week of rollout, and document the 5-10 most common workflows in a shared place your team can reference.

The teams that get the most value out of Informatica CDQ treat the first month as a structured rollout, not an experiment. Set a clear goal (what should this tool be doing for us by week 4?), measure against it, and adjust before you commit to an annual contract.
