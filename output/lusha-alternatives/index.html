Best Lusha Alternatives (2026)

Lusha is great for quick contact lookups, but its database is smaller than ZoomInfo's and it can't do waterfall enrichment like Clay. These alternatives offer more depth.

The most common complaints about Lusha include smaller database than zoominfo, limited enrichment beyond basic contact data, credit limits on lower tiers. These aren't minor gripes. They're the kind of friction that pushes teams to evaluate other options after months of trying to make it work.

Lusha scores 7.2/10 in our review, which means it's a solid tool for its target audience. But if you're not in that target audience (Individual reps and small teams wanting quick contact lookups from LinkedIn), you'll feel the gaps quickly.

The Bottom Line

If you're leaving Lusha, Clay is the strongest overall replacement. It scores 8.2/10 and starts at $149/mo. Apollo.io is a close second, especially if you're looking for smbs and startups wanting prospecting data + engagement in one affordable platform. Don't just pick the highest-scored option though. Match the tool to your workflow, your team size, and your budget. The best Lusha alternative is the one that solves the specific problem that made you start looking in the first place.

What is the best Lusha alternative in 2026?

The Sultan's top pick is Clay (scored 8.2/10). It offers a strong feature set at a competitive price point starting at $149/mo.

Why do people switch away from Lusha?

Common reasons include pricing changes, missing features for specific workflows, and team size constraints. Lusha scores 7.2/10 overall, which means there are clear areas where alternatives can outperform it.

Is it hard to migrate away from Lusha?

Most Lusha alternatives offer import tools or migration guides. Plan for 1-3 weeks depending on your data volume and the number of integrations you need to reconnect.
