The Sultan's Verdict

HubSpot's free tier and ecosystem breadth beat Close for most teams. Close wins decisively for phone-heavy outbound teams, though. Its built-in dialer and calling workflows are better than anything HubSpot offers without add-ons.

Pricing Deep Dive

HubSpot CRM offers 4 tiers: Free at $0, Starter at $20/mo, Professional at $500/mo, Enterprise at $1,200/mo. The entry point sits at Free / $20/mo, which lands it in the free range for this category.

Close prices across 3 tiers: Startup at $49/user/mo, Professional at $99/user/mo, Enterprise at $139/user/mo. Starting at $49/user/mo, it falls into the mid bracket.

When you stack the two side by side, HubSpot CRM at Free / $20/mo vs Close at $49/user/mo, the question isn't which costs less. It's which gives you more per dollar for your specific workflow. A cheaper tool that doesn't fit your process costs more in lost productivity than a pricier one that does.
