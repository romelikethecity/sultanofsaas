Modjo Pricing (2026)

Starting at Custom

Pricing for Modjo begins at Custom. That's on the expensive side compared to the rest of the conversation intelligence space. Our review gives it a 6.0/10. Below is the tier-by-tier breakdown, the math for different team sizes, and the parts of the pricing model that deserve closer scrutiny before you commit.

The quick read on Modjo: European conversation intelligence platform. Strong in EU markets with GDPR-compliant architecture and multi-language transcription. In English-speaking markets, Gong and Sybill are stronger choices. Pick Modjo if you need native European language support and EU data residency.

What's Included in Modjo Pricing

Every plan includes the core Modjo feature set. Here's what you get access to on paid tiers:

  • Call recording
  • Multi-language transcription
  • Coaching
  • Deal intelligence
  • CRM integration
  • EU data residency

Feature depth grows with the tier. Entry plans cap on automation, integrations, or usage limits. Upper plans unlock the heavier features that mid-market teams actually need. Read the vendor's feature matrix before picking a tier, especially if one specific feature is the reason you're buying.

What to Watch Out For

The most common pricing complaints buyers raise about Modjo:

  • Custom pricing is opaque
  • Weaker in English-only markets
  • Smaller feature set than Gong

None of these are deal-breakers on their own. They're the things you want to negotiate or plan around before you sign a contract. The worst time to discover an add-on fee is month three.

The Sultan's Verdict on Modjo Pricing

Modjo scores 6.0/10, which is a reminder that the price tag isn't the whole picture. You're paying Custom for a product with real limitations, and the cons matter. Before committing, check the alternatives above. At this score, you need a specific reason to pick Modjo over the leaders in conversation intelligence.

The fit test is simple. Modjo is built for european sales teams needing gdpr-compliant ci with multi-language support. If that's you, the pricing is worth it. If it's not, you'll end up paying for features you never touch while missing features you actually need. Buy the tool that fits your motion, not the one with the best pricing page.

The bottom line: Modjo's pricing is defensible if you actually use what it's good at. Its biggest strength is gdpr-compliant european data residency, and that's where the money goes. If that strength maps to a real pain point in your business, pay the price. If not, walk away and pick something cheaper.

Modjo Pricing FAQs

How much does Modjo cost?

Modjo starts at Custom. The paid plans scale up from there based on features, seats, or usage. Check the pricing table above for the full tier breakdown.

Does Modjo have a free trial?

Modjo doesn't lead with a free plan, so check the vendor site for current trial terms. Most tools in this category offer a 14-day trial, and some let you demo the product before signing up.

Are there hidden costs with Modjo?

The biggest gotcha buyers report: custom pricing is opaque. Read the contract line items before signing, and ask for the full cost including onboarding and add-ons.

Does Modjo offer annual discounts?

Expect a 15-20% cut for annual prepay, which is standard in this category. Ask sales directly and push for more if you're committing to 10+ seats or multiple years. Monthly billing is the wrong move if you already know you'll stick around.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Modjo?

Yes. Fireflies.ai starts at Free / $10/user/mo, which undercuts Modjo's Custom. It's a lighter product in some areas, so audit the feature gap before switching. Our Fireflies.ai review covers the trade-offs.
