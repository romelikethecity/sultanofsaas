Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-30

SultanOfSaaS ("we," "us," "our") operates the website sultanofsaas.com. This page describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

If you contact us by email, we collect your email address and the content of your message. We do not require account creation, login, or payment to access any content on the Site.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit sultanofsaas.com, we may collect standard web analytics data through Google Analytics 4 (GA4), including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (city/region level, not precise)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is aggregated and used to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use this data to identify individual visitors.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for these purposes:

  • Site improvement: Analytics data helps us understand which reviews and categories are most useful and where to focus our efforts.
  • Communication: If you email us directly, we use your email to respond to your message.

Email Service

If we send transactional emails (such as responses to inquiries), they are sent through Resend. Resend processes your email address solely to deliver our messages. Their privacy policy is available at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Analytics (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 for aggregate site analytics. GA4 collects anonymized usage data to help us understand traffic patterns and content performance. We do not use analytics data for advertising, retargeting, or any form of individual tracking. Google's privacy policy.

Cookies

We use cookies only through Google Analytics 4 for aggregate site analytics. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking across sites, or personalizing content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4: Web analytics. Google's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting. GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Google Fonts: Web fonts. Google Fonts privacy FAQ.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics 4 default settings (14 months). We do not maintain databases of visitor personal information.

Your Rights

To request information about any personal data we may hold, or to request its deletion, email hello@sultanofsaas.com and we will process your request within 48 hours.

If you are located in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@sultanofsaas.com to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

SultanOfSaaS is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We will not materially reduce your privacy protections without notice.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email hello@sultanofsaas.com.
