Best HubSpot CRM Alternatives (2026)

HubSpot's paid tiers can get expensive fast. If the free CRM isn't enough but the paid plans strain your budget, these alternatives deliver similar value.

The most common complaints about HubSpot CRM include paid tiers get expensive quickly, per-seat pricing adds up for larger teams, some features locked behind top tiers. These aren't minor gripes. They're the kind of friction that pushes teams to evaluate other options after months of trying to make it work.

HubSpot CRM scores 8.9/10 in our review, which means it's a solid tool for its target audience. But if you're not in that target audience (SMBs who want a CRM they can start using today without paying a dime), you'll feel the gaps quickly.

The Bottom Line

If you're leaving HubSpot CRM, Pipedrive is the strongest overall replacement. It scores 8.2/10 and starts at $14/user/mo. Close is a close second, especially if you're looking for inside sales teams running high-volume outbound. Don't just pick the highest-scored option though. Match the tool to your workflow, your team size, and your budget. The best HubSpot CRM alternative is the one that solves the specific problem that made you start looking in the first place.

What is the best HubSpot CRM alternative in 2026?

The Sultan's top pick is Pipedrive (scored 8.2/10). It offers a strong feature set at a competitive price point starting at $14/user/mo.

Why do people switch away from HubSpot CRM?

Common reasons include pricing changes, missing features for specific workflows, and team size constraints. HubSpot CRM scores 8.9/10 overall, which means there are clear areas where alternatives can outperform it.

Is it hard to migrate away from HubSpot CRM?

Most HubSpot CRM alternatives offer import tools or migration guides. Plan for 1-3 weeks depending on your data volume and the number of integrations you need to reconnect.
