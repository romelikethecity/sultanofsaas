The Sultan's Verdict

Gong leads on conversation analytics and coaching. Clari leads on pipeline forecasting and deal risk scoring. Both are premium tools. If your priority is coaching reps, Gong. If your priority is forecasting revenue, Clari. For pure conversation intelligence, Gong is the category leader.

Pricing Deep Dive

Clari offers 3 tiers: Standard at $40/user/mo, Professional at $65/user/mo, Enterprise at Custom. The entry point sits at $40/user/mo, which lands it in the mid range for this category.

Gong prices across 3 tiers: Standard at ~$100/user/mo, Premium at ~$130/user/mo, Enterprise at Custom. Starting at $100/user/mo, it falls into the premium bracket.

When you stack the two side by side, Clari at $40/user/mo vs Gong at $100/user/mo, the question isn't which costs less. It's which gives you more per dollar for your specific workflow. A cheaper tool that doesn't fit your process costs more in lost productivity than a pricier one that does.
