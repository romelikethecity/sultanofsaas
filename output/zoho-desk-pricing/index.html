Zoho Desk Pricing (2026)

Starting at Free / $7/agent/mo

Pricing for Zoho Desk begins at Free / $7/agent/mo. That's one of the cheaper entry points compared to the rest of the help desk space. Our review gives it a 7.0/10. Below is the tier-by-tier breakdown, the math for different team sizes, and the parts of the pricing model that deserve closer scrutiny before you commit.

The quick read on Zoho Desk: Affordable help desk from the Zoho ecosystem. Good feature set for the price, but the UI is a step behind Freshdesk. Best value if you are already invested in Zoho's suite.

Zoho Desk Plans Explained

Breaking down every Zoho Desk plan, what's included, and who should pay for it.

Free — $0 (3 agents)

The free plan is the honest starting point. You can set up Zoho Desk, connect it to your workflow, and get real use out of it without handing over a credit card. For solo founders and tiny teams, this is often all you need for the first 6-12 months.

Express — $7/agent/mo

At $7/mo, Express is the tier Zoho Desk wants you to pick. It fills the gaps in the entry plan and adds the integrations and automation that most teams discover they need in week two. Budget for this from day one if you're serious about using Zoho Desk.

Standard — $14/agent/mo

Standard costs $14/mo. It's a step-up from the base plan, typically with more automation, higher limits, or advanced integrations. Worth it only if you can point to a specific feature on this tier that you actually need.

Professional — $23/agent/mo

At $23/mo, Professional is the ceiling of the self-serve pricing. It bundles in the features teams ask for after they hit scale. Good value if you actually use them, expensive padding if you don't.

What's Included in Zoho Desk Pricing

Every plan includes the core Zoho Desk feature set. Here's what you get access to on paid tiers:

  • Ticketing
  • Knowledge base
  • AI assistant
  • Community forums
  • Telephony
  • SLA management

Feature depth grows with the tier. Entry plans cap on automation, integrations, or usage limits. Upper plans unlock the heavier features that mid-market teams actually need. Read the vendor's feature matrix before picking a tier, especially if one specific feature is the reason you're buying.

What to Watch Out For

The most common pricing complaints buyers raise about Zoho Desk:

  • Can feel clunky
  • UI lags behind Freshdesk
  • Limited outside Zoho ecosystem

None of these are deal-breakers on their own. They're the things you want to negotiate or plan around before you sign a contract. The worst time to discover an add-on fee is month three.

The Sultan's Verdict on Zoho Desk Pricing

Zoho Desk scores 7.0/10, which is a reminder that the price tag isn't the whole picture. You're paying Free / $7/agent/mo for a product with real limitations, and the cons matter. Before committing, check the alternatives above. At this score, you need a specific reason to pick Zoho Desk over the leaders in help desk.

The fit test is simple. Zoho Desk is built for teams using other zoho products who want a unified ecosystem. If that's you, the pricing is worth it. If it's not, you'll end up paying for features you never touch while missing features you actually need. Buy the tool that fits your motion, not the one with the best pricing page.

The bottom line: Zoho Desk's pricing is defensible if you actually use what it's good at. Its biggest strength is very affordable, and that's where the money goes. If that strength maps to a real pain point in your business, pay the price. If not, walk away and pick something cheaper.

Zoho Desk Pricing FAQs

How much does Zoho Desk cost?

Zoho Desk has a free plan, and the first paid tier is Express at $7/agent/mo. Most teams that outgrow the free tier end up on Express or higher once they hit the free-plan limits.

Is there a free version of Zoho Desk?

Yes. Zoho Desk offers a free plan that covers the basics. It's a real product, not a time-limited trial, so you can run on it indefinitely if your needs stay small.

Does Zoho Desk charge per user?

No, Zoho Desk's Express plan is a flat platform fee of $7/agent/mo. You can add team members without the per-seat pricing that most SaaS tools use.

Are there hidden costs with Zoho Desk?

The biggest gotcha buyers report: can feel clunky. Read the contract line items before signing, and ask for the full cost including onboarding and add-ons.

Is Zoho Desk cheaper if you pay annually?

Yes, like most tools in this space, Zoho Desk typically discounts annual plans by 15-20%. If the public page only shows monthly, email sales and ask. Founders on tight runway should take the annual cut; everyone else should still consider it.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Zoho Desk?

Freshdesk is the budget alternative worth looking at first. It begins at Free / $15/agent/mo compared to Zoho Desk's Free / $7/agent/mo. Feature parity isn't perfect, so read the Freshdesk review before switching.
